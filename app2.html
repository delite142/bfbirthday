My Favourite Food

Fried plantain and fried egg is one of my favourite meals. It is easy to make and tastes really good. This meal can be eaten at any time either in the morning, afternoon, evening.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe plantains
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion
  • Seasoning cube
  • Oil
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Remove the skin and cut into thin slices, either round or slanted into a bowl and sprinkle salt.
  2. Pour oil into a pan and let it heat up on medium heat and fry it till golden brown.
  3. break the eggs into a bowl, add chopped onions, peppers and a pinch of seasoning. Whisk
  4. heat up a lille oil in a pan, pour in the egg mixture. Serve
